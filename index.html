Steve Jobs Timeline

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco, California.
  • 1972 - Jobs enrolled at Reed College in Portland, Oregon.
  • 1975 - Starts working on Apple I Computer together with Steve Wozniak.
  • 1976 - Jobs, Wozniak and Wayne founded Apple Computer Company.
  • 1976 - Apple I went on sale.
  • 1983 - Macintosh is launched at Apple's annual shareholder meeting.
  • 1985 - Jobs left Apple and started a new computer firm NeXT.
  • 1986 - Buys Pixar Animation Studios.
  • 1991 - Steve Jobs marries Laurene Powel.
  • 1993 - Toy Story work is haulted and becomes NeXT Software Inc.
  • 1996 - Apple buys NeXT $400M.
  • 2000 - Steve Jobs is back as CEO at Apple Inc.
  • 2001 - First iPod is introduced and Apple opens retail stores.
  • 2007 - Introduces iPhone, Apple TV and iPod Touch.
  • 2007 - Apple Computer becomes Apple Inc.
  • 2009 - Jobs takes medical leave for six months and has surgery for liver transplant.
  • 2010 - Introduces iPad, iPhone 4, iPad Touch 4G and new MacBook Air.
  • 2011 - Resigned his position as CEO of Apple.
  • 2011 - On 5th of October 2011 Steve Jobs passes away.
